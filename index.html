ABOUT ME

I am sarvel :wave:. This is how I look.

20yo with unhealthy obsession about computer architecture and system design. I have designed 5 ISAs /*I think*/ and implemented them in at least 9 ways as digital simulation or on hardware.

Currently I am working on p16b4x which is probably final ISA from 16b "family" that I worked on, later I plan on just expanding it indefinitely. //Which is one of the reasons why it is taking this long to even get it to working state.
Concurrently I am working on mALUch which is yet another 16bit ISA, this one is developed as part of my Uni Student Club. //More details soon!

My present long term plans are to bring at least simple implementations of p16b4x to ASIC and be able to use it as fully functional multi user computer.

I am a full time student of Electronics and Telecommunications.

My "minor" interests which I pursued to some degree include: ASICs, FPGAs, high performance computing, electronics in general, operating systems, programming languages, math, algorithms, and physics. Other than that I enjoy biking, running, and recently cooking.


If you are interested in anything I wrote above or broadly speaking low level programming, hit me up!!
